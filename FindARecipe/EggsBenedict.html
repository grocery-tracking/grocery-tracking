Eggs Benedict

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Difficulty Level: Executive Chef

Ingredients:

Two whole eggs

Four strips of cooked bacon

One cup of hollandaise sauce

One cup of spinach

Procedure:

1. Crack one egg in a measuring cup. Lower it into boiling water, and ease it out of the cup.

2. Repeat with other eggs. Cook each egg 4-5 minutes until poached.

3. Sautee spinach.

4. Cut each bacon strip into 3 pieces.

5. Place pieces of bacon and spinach on top of each poached egg.

6. Add a dollop of hollandaise sauce over the top.
